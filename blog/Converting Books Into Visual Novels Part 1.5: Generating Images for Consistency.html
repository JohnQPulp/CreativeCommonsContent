Image Type Recap

The three image "types" that the site currently supports are backgrounds, "cards", and character sprites. Here's an example with all three:

Backgrounds occupy the full background of the VN window. Cards sit on top of the background in the middle of the screen, and character sprites sit on top of the background either in the lower-left speaker corner or on the screen if meant to be set into the background.

With these images, the goal is to have them look good, clean, text-accurate, and importantly: consistent. Inconsistencies in art styles are very distracting, and so we want the style of the lines, the colors, the hatching shading, and everything else to sell the idea that these images all exist together.

The above example I think does okay, but the central card looks a little off, which they tend to do pretty often, and so cards aren't used very often. So instead, let's mainly focus on the two main types of images: character sprites and backgrounds.
